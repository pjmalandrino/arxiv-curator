About ArXiv Curator

An intelligent system that automatically discovers, analyzes, and summarizes the latest AI research papers to keep you at the forefront of innovation

Our Mission

The field of AI research moves at breakneck speed, with hundreds of papers published daily. ArXiv Curator helps researchers, engineers, and enthusiasts stay informed by automatically processing and summarizing the most relevant papers in machine learning, natural language processing, and artificial intelligence.

How It Works

  1. Discovery: The pipeline queries ArXiv for recent papers in AI/ML categories
  2. Filtering: Papers are filtered based on configured keywords and relevance
  3. Analysis: Each paper is processed by the LLM to extract insights
  4. Storage: Papers and summaries are stored in PostgreSQL
  5. Presentation: Results are displayed in this beautiful web interface
